- **Secondary:** Purple accents (#d946ef to #c026d3)
- **Accent:** Orange highlights (#f97316 to #ea580c)

- **Headlines:** Inter Bold, 32-48px
- **Body text:** Inter Regular, 16-20px
- **Captions:** Inter Medium, 14px
